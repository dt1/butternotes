Izotope Vinyl (Free Plugin)

Quite often, you'll hear someone say she uses Izotope Vinyl on "everything," and it's not hard to understand why this may be the case, if one is so inclined to add a bit of dusty vintage to her tracks.

This plugin has a wide variety of warped vinyl, ranging from completely busted to a soft bit of warp. Another feature is the spin down button, which sounds like someone is stopping the record without taking the needle off.

Each button and slider does exactly what it advertises, and the user interface is simple enough that anyone can look at it and figure out what it does.

What isn't obvious is the high quality audio that this plugin creates. If you aren't familiar with the Izotope collection of plugins, this is a great entry into the company's products. This is one of the few free plugins that is sigificantly better than stock.

For the price of $0, you really can't go wrong by downloading this.

This plugin is available in VST, VST3, 64-bit AAX, AU, and 32-bit RTAS.
